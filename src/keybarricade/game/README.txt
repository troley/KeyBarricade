How to play the KeyBarricade:

- Move: up, down, left, right arrow keys.

- Pick up key: E.

- Use key: Q.

- Restart level: R.

- Exit game: Esc.

The player can move around, pick up keys with id's and open barricades with
matching id's. The player should get to the green tile which indicates finish
and gets the player to the next level.
